king prawn linguine

Linguine with tomatoes, greens, and prawns in a buttery sauce.

    Ingredients

  • 450 g linguine
  • 1 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 450 g prawns, peeled and deveined
  • salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 tbsp. butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 600 g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 60 ml white wine
  • 50 g freshly grated Parmesan, plus more for garnish
  • 240 ml double cream

Jollof Rice

Jollof rice is a very common dish around africa and is filled with spices

    Ingredients

  • 2 Cup Rice, washed
  • 1-2 Cups Stock (Chicken, Beef, Goat)
  • 1 Chopped Onion
  • 2 Cups Water
  • 1 tsp Salt (Depending on stock)
  • 2 Seasoning Cubes (Depending on stock)
  • 5 Plum Tomatoes
  • 2 Tatashe
  • 2 Habanero Pepper
  • 1 tsp Cameroon Pepper
  • 3 tbs Tomato Puree (Tinned Tomato)
  • 1 tbs thyme
  • 3 Bayleafs
  • 1 Cup Vegetable Oil
